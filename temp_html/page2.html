The MDCATEMY Scholarships

We know that not everyone can afford MDCATEMY. We do not want money to be the reason a deserving student misses their shot at becoming a doctor.

That is why we are offering 20 scholarships for Bahadur Batch 2026. These are need-based scholarships for students who have worked hard academically and genuinely need financial support to access the right preparation.

To apply you must have:

  • Above 90% in Matric
  • Above 90% in FSc or FSc 1st year (if 2nd year result is not yet out)
  • Genuine financial need

Application Process

Step 1

Fill the Scholarship Form

The form will ask for your personal details, academic results, and required documents. Fill it completely and honestly. Incomplete forms will not be reviewed.

Step 2

Record a 5 Minute Video

Record a maximum 5 minute video introducing yourself and answering these three questions:

  • Why do you deserve a spot in Bahadur Batch?
  • Why do you need a scholarship financially?
  • What are your goals and ambitions behind cracking the MDCAT?

Paste the video link in the scholarship form before submitting.

Note: Male students must show their faces in the video. Female students can wear abaya, hijab, or niqab and if they are uncomfortable with a video, they can record a voice message instead.

Step 3

Wait for Our Response

We will personally review every application and reach out to shortlisted students directly on WhatsApp.

Apply for the MDCATEMY Scholarship

20 scholarships will be awarded Inshallah.
Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis so apply as early as possible. Only students who meet the academic eligibility criteria and submit a complete form with a video will be considered.
